From the early 80s to the late 90s, we Peruvians experienced a pivotal internal armed conflict between the government and the Communist Party of Peru -Shining Path. This StoryMap encompasses the geolocations of the students’ whereabouts from the instance they were taken by the military and finally disappeared in the emblematic mass grave known as La Hoyada in Huamanga, Ayacucho (Peru). This work was drawn from the solid research developed by the Directorate General of Search for Disappeared Persons (DGBPD) promoting routes for more narratives of the unofficial collective memory.
